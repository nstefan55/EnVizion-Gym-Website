What We Offer ?

24/7 Access

Unlock the convenience of round the clock fitness with our 24/7 access feature, providing members the flexibility to work out on their schedule, anytime, day or night.

Personal Training Sessions

Experience personalized fitness guidance and accelerated results with our one-on-one personal training sessions, tailored to your unique goals and abilities.

Steam Room and Sauna

Indulge in relaxation and wellness with our steam room and sauna facilities, offering a tranquil retreat to rejuvenate both body and mind.

Basketball Court

Elevate your game on our state-of-the-art basketball court, where athleticism meets camaraderie in an energizing and dynamic environment.

Table-Tennis Playground

Experience the fast-paced thrill of table tennis on our dedicated courts, where skill-building, friendly matches, and a lively atmosphere create the perfect setting for both casual play and competitive excitement.

Top of the line Equipment

Unleash your potential with the best in fitness with our top tier equipment that provides the ultimate platform for achieving your wellness goals with precision and excellence.

Indoor Pool

Dive into luxury and well-being in our indoor pool oasis, offering a serene environment for lap swims, aqua workouts, and relaxation in a temperature-controlled setting.

Refer-a-Friend Bonus

Refer a friend, and both of you receive a free personal training session.

Student Bonus

Special 10% off on all membership types for first 3 months for students with valid ID

Weekend Warrior Pass

Unlimited access to the gym on weekends at a reduced rate.

Group Training Sessions

Fuel your motivation with invigorating group training sessions, where camaraderie and expert guidance combine for dynamic workouts that inspire and elevate your fitness journey.
